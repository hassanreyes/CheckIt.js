Upload your checklists is easy; remember they must be written in plain text (.txt) and match the following rules. Yuo can also check this sample file.

  • The first line is a metadata and is enclosure in curly brackets {}. It follows the standard json format, the possible fields are:
    • language: The written language of the checklist (see the languages list)
    • author: The name of the original author if known.
    • reference: The website link or any other reference of the origin of the checklist, if exit.
    Note: enclosure all values in double quotes.
  • The second line is the Checklist title. The title must be just one line.
  • The following consecutive lines are the checklist description. The description ends until a blank line is left. This is optional.
  • After title and description, all lines after a black line and that had no tab at the font are consider section's title.
  • The next consecutive line after a section's title will be taken as the sections description.
  • All lines below a section and after a blank line and begins with a tabulation are consider section´s items. All items can be as long as yuo want and they only ends until a blank line is left.
    • Note: Any checklist must have at least one section. Items can't be orphans.
